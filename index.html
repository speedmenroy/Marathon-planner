Post-Race Recovery
First 30 min: Walk 10 min, hydrate (500ml), eat carbs + protein.
1–2h: Light static stretching, cold shower or ice bath 10–15 min.
Day 1–3: Active recovery (easy walk/swim), elevation, compression.
Week 1–2: No hard running — easy jogs only, focus on sleep & nutrition.
Nutrition Guide
Fueling for plant-based runners
🏃 Base Phase
Carbs: 5–7g / kg
Protein: 1.6g / kg
Fat: 1g / kg
Focus on aerobic base; adequate recovery nutrition.
⬆️ Build Phase
Carbs: 7–9g / kg
Protein: 1.8g / kg
Fat: 1g / kg
Increased training load; prioritize carb availability.
🔥 Peak Phase
Carbs: 8–10g / kg
Protein: 2g / kg
Fat: 0.8g / kg
Maximum volume; carbohydrate dominant. Sleep 8–9h.
📉 Taper Phase
Carbs: Reduce 10–15%
Protein: Maintain 2g / kg
Fat: 1g / kg
Reduce volume with training; maintain protein to preserve muscle.
🏆 Race Day Nutrition
Pre-Race Breakfast (3h before)
  • 🌾 3g/kg bodyweight carbohydrates
  • 🍌 Banana + oatmeal + toast
  • ☕ Light coffee (optional, tested in training)
  • 💧 500ml water with meal + 200ml 30min before
During Race Fueling
  • 🧃 Gel or 2 dates every 45 minutes
  • 💧 150–200ml water at every aid station
  • 🍌 Banana at 30km mark (real food option)
  • 🧂 Electrolyte drink if sweating heavily
🌅 Pre-Run Fuels
  • 🌾 Oats with banana + almond butter
  • 🍞 Toast with peanut butter + banana
  • 🌴 Dates (3–4) + handful of nuts
  • 🍚 Rice balls with sesame + pickled plum
  • 🥣 Granola with soy milk
🏃 During Run
  • 🧃 Energy gels (plant-based)
  • 🌴 Medjool dates (2–3 per 45 min)
  • 🍌 Banana halves at aid stations
  • 🍚 Rice balls for ultras
  • 💧 Coconut water (natural electrolytes)
🌙 Post-Run Recovery
  • 🥚 Tofu scramble + veggies
  • 🍲 Lentil soup + crusty bread
  • 🍛 Chickpea curry + basmati rice
  • 🥛 Greek yogurt + protein + berries
  • 🌿 Quinoa bowls + roasted veggies

Meal Timing
PRE-RUN
2–3h before: Full meal. 30–60min: Light snack only (banana, dates).
POST-RUN
Within 30 min: 3:1 carb:protein ratio. E.g. tofu + rice bowl.
NIGHT BEFORE RACE
Carb-load with pasta, rice, or grain bowl. Avoid new foods.

Integrations
Connect your favorite running platforms
All integrations currently require manual sync. Use the export/import tips below for each platform.
🟠
Strava
The world's most popular running social platform. Track, analyze, and share your activities.
⚠️ Manual sync only
💡 Export GPX from Strava Activity → import to PaceForge calendar manually. Visit strava.com
🔵
Garmin Connect
Professional-grade GPS watch data with advanced metrics like training load, recovery advisor, and VO2 max.
⚠️ Manual sync only
💡 Export .fit or .tcx from Garmin Connect → use Garmin's activity export to log workouts manually. Visit connect.garmin.com
⚫
Nike Run Club
Guided runs, coaching plans, and global community challenges powered by Nike's training ecosystem.
⚠️ Manual sync only
💡 View run history in NRC app → manually log distance and pace into PaceForge. Visit nike.com/nrc-app
❤️
Apple Health
Comprehensive health and fitness tracking on iPhone and Apple Watch. Integrates with hundreds of apps.
⚠️ Manual sync only
💡 Export Health data (XML) from iPhone Settings → parse workouts using third-party tools. Visit apple.com/health
🗺️
MapMyRun
Route mapping and tracking by Under Armour. Great for discovering and creating running routes.
⚠️ Manual sync only
💡 Export workout CSV from MapMyRun training log → reference it to manually add workouts here. Visit mapmyrun.com
